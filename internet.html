Everybody is connected

Below is a data visualization of the global internet users dataset. The map shows the percentange of internet users in the year 2020, and the line chart on the right shows the change across time.
If you hover over the map you will be able to see the trend change on the line chart. If you select a country from the legend you'll see the trend for that country and the percentange on the map.
For the countries that have no internet users in 2020, you will not be able to see the change reflected on the map.
