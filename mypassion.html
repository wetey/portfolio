The data visual is from FourWeekMBA's article on Microsoft's business model. The aim of this visual is to show Microsoft's business model and show the revenue made from each category from 2019 to 2023. The unpaid intern who was probably rushing to meet a deadline generated a plot that has a lot of overlapping text and is overall difficult to read. Specially when the lines are close together, it's difficult to know which number belongs to which product line.

The visual above is the improved version of the original plot. We can clearly see for each year, what the revenue from each category was. The bar graphs are able to display the information for the categories that had similar or equal revenue better than the line graph. In addition, there is no overlapping text and we can easily see which category is which. The legend is placed next to the plot as opposed to directly on the plot like the original plot.

The data sources are from Microsoft annual report from the year 2019 to 2023. Reports can be found here: 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023
